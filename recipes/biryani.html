Biryani

Description

Biryani is a bit of a project and is time-consuming, but I have never been disappointed with the results. Since it is such a festive dish, I usually make it for dinner parties. It is a huge hit.

  • Cooking oil
  • Whole cloves

  • Cinnamon sticks
  • Onions

  • Ginger paste
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Fresh mint
  • chicken chops
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Green chile peppers
  • Ground red pepper
  • Plain yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Water
  • Vegetable oil
  • Saffron
